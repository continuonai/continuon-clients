### Mounting Options

#### Option A: Velcro + Strap (Recommended - Easy & Secure)

| Item | Purpose | Amazon Link | Price |
|------|---------|-------------|-------|
| 3M Dual Lock (1" × 10ft) | Bottom adhesion | B07D2M6Z21 | ~$10 |
| Ratchet/Velcro Strap | Top retention | B07TVRXQPF | ~$8 |

**Installation:**
1. Clean chassis surface and EcoFlow bottom with isopropyl alcohol
2. Apply 4× strips of Dual Lock (corners of EcoFlow footprint)
3. Press EcoFlow firmly onto chassis (Dual Lock engages)
4. Run strap over top of EcoFlow through chassis holes/slots
5. Tighten strap securely

**Advantages:**
- Quick removal for charging
- No drilling required
- Vibration dampening

#### Option B: L-Bracket Retention (Maximum Security)

| Item | Purpose | Amazon Link | Price |
|------|---------|-------------|-------|
| Aluminum L-Brackets (4pk) | Corner retention | B07GWPZTCS | ~$12 |
| M5×10mm Bolts | Bracket to chassis | (included in kit) | - |

**Installation:**
1. Position EcoFlow centered on chassis
2. Mark L-bracket positions at 4 corners
3. Drill/use existing holes in chassis
4. Bolt L-brackets to chassis (brackets touch EcoFlow sides)
5. EcoFlow is captive but can slide up for removal

### Assembly Steps

#### Step 1: Prepare V-Slot Uprights
1. Insert 2× M5 T-nuts into the bottom of each V-slot upright
2. Position T-nuts in the channel that will face the chassis

#### Step 2: Attach Corner Brackets to Chassis
1. Position brackets at rear corners of chassis
2. Spacing: ~240mm apart (uprights straddle EcoFlow)
3. Mark hole positions on chassis
4. Use existing holes or drill M5 holes
5. Bolt brackets to chassis (don't fully tighten yet)

#### Step 3: Mount Uprights to Brackets
1. Slide V-slot uprights into corner brackets
2. Align T-nuts with bracket holes
3. Insert M5 bolts through bracket into T-nuts
4. Check vertical alignment with level/square
5. Tighten all bolts

#### Step 4: Add Top Cross-Brace
1. Cut V-slot 2020 to 320mm (or order pre-cut)
2. Insert T-nuts into uprights at top
3. Connect cross-brace to both uprights
4. This rigidizes the entire mast structure

### Alignment Tips

```
Checking Vertical Alignment:

      ┌─┐ ← Use carpenter's square
      │ │   or phone level app
      │ │
      │ │  Upright should be
      │ │  90° to chassis
      │ │
══════╧══════
   Chassis
```

- Use a carpenter's square or level app on phone
- Adjust bracket position before final tightening
- Both uprights must be parallel to each other
- Cross-brace helps lock alignment

## 3. Complete Assembly Order

1. **Chassis preparation**
   - Unbox and inspect chassis
   - Identify mounting hole patterns
   - Clean surfaces for adhesive

2. **EcoFlow mounting**
   - Apply Dual Lock strips to chassis
   - Position and press EcoFlow
   - Add retention strap

3. **Corner bracket installation**
   - Position brackets behind EcoFlow
   - Bolt to chassis (leave slightly loose)

4. **V-slot upright mounting**
   - Insert T-nuts into uprights
   - Slide into corner brackets
   - Check alignment, tighten

5. **Cross-brace installation**
   - Connect top of uprights
   - Final alignment check

6. **Wiring routing**
   - Plan cable paths along uprights
   - Use cable clips in V-slot channels

## 6. Troubleshooting

### EcoFlow Slides During Movement
- Add more Dual Lock strips
- Tighten retention strap
- Consider L-bracket method instead

### Mast Wobbles
- Check corner bracket tightness
- Verify cross-brace is secure
- Add diagonal bracing if needed

### Uprights Not Parallel
- Loosen all bolts
- Use straight edge across both uprights
- Re-tighten in sequence (bottom first, then top)

### Can't Find Chassis Mounting Holes
- Many chassis have slotted holes for flexibility
- Drill new M5 holes if needed (mark carefully)
- Use self-tapping screws as alternative
